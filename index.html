College Rules & Guidelines

Attendance Policy

  • Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory
  • Medical certificates must be submitted within 3 days
  • Parents will be notified if attendance falls below 60%

Dress Code

  • Formal attire is mandatory on all working days
  • ID cards must be worn at all times
  • Lab coat compulsory during practical sessions

Academic Rules

  • Internal assessments are mandatory
  • Assignments must be submitted on time
  • Maintain discipline during lectures

Campus Guidelines

  • No smoking within campus premises
  • Mobile phones must be silent in academic areas
  • Maintain cleanliness in the campus
